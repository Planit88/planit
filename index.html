How to use Planit

Ireland's campervan community

🗺 Finding spots

Browse the map and tap any pin to see details about a spot. Numbered circles are clusters — tap them to zoom in and see individual spots. Use the Filter button to search by type, rating or cost.

⭐ Rating & reviewing

Open any spot and scroll down to leave a star rating and written review. Your review is anonymous — no account needed.

➕ Sharing a spot

Tap the orange + Share a spot button, then tap the map where the spot is located. Fill in the name, type, cost and any amenities you know about. Your spot appears for everyone instantly.

❤️ Favourites

Tap the heart icon on any spot to save it to your favourites. Filter by My Favourites to see them all. Favourites are saved on your device.

📍 Getting directions

Open any spot and tap Get Directions to open it in Google Maps with turn-by-turn directions to the spot.

🚩 Reporting issues

If a spot is wrong, no longer exists or has inappropriate content, tap 🚩 Report this spot at the bottom of the detail sheet. Our team will review it.

📍 Your location

Tap the compass icon on the map to show your current location as a blue dot. This helps you find spots near you.
