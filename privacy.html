Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Planit is a community map for sharing sites, pubs and parking spots across Ireland. We are committed to being transparent about what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Who we are

Planit is operated as a community project. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, you can contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

2. What data we collect

Planit collects only the information that users deliberately submit when adding a spot to the map:

  • Spot name — the name you give to a location
  • Location coordinates — the latitude and longitude of where you tap on the map
  • Notes — any description or tips you choose to write
  • Type, cost and amenities — the category and features you select
  • Star rating — any rating you submit for a spot
  • Timestamp — the date and time a spot was added

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, IP address, or any other personal information. We do not use cookies, analytics, or any form of tracking.

3. How your data is used

All submitted spot data is displayed publicly on the Planit map so that other users can discover and visit those locations. This is the sole purpose for which data is collected.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

4. Where your data is stored

Spot data is stored using Google Firebase Firestore, with data hosted in the European Union (Belgium). Google Firebase is subject to Google's privacy policy and data processing terms, which are compliant with GDPR. You can read more at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

If Firebase has not been configured, data is stored locally on your own device only and is not shared with anyone.

5. Your rights under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right to deletion — you can delete any spot you added at any time using the "Delete this spot" button on the spot's detail screen within the app.
  • Right to access — you can request a copy of any data associated with spots you submitted.
  • Right to rectification — if you believe data you submitted is incorrect, you can contact us to have it corrected or removed.
  • Right to object — you can contact us at any time to request removal of data you submitted.

To exercise any of these rights, use the delete button in the app or contact us directly.

6. Important notice about location data

Please do not submit the exact location of private property, your home, or any location that could identify a private individual. All spot data is publicly visible to all users of Planit.

7. Map data

Map tiles are provided by OpenFreeMap, which uses data from OpenStreetMap. OpenFreeMap does not collect user data or use cookies. Map data is © OpenStreetMap contributors.

8. Children's privacy

Planit is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.
